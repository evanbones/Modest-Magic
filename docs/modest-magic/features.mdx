This mod introduces a simple yet powerful ritual crafting system centered around the **Altar** and **Pedestals**.

With these blocks, you can infuse items with magic, apply powerful enchantments, and even summon entities.

### Features
* Ritual Crafting - Place items on Pedestals around a central Altar to perform magical rituals.
* Three new recipe types:
  * Infusing - Transform items into new, magical variants.
  * Enchanting - Apply specific pools of enchantments to your gear.
  * Summoning - Bring entities to life by sacrificing items.
* Altars and Pedstals are compatible with hoppers, pipes, and redstone for automation.
* All three recipe types are fully compatible with EMI for browsing ritual recipes in-game.
